\section{Problem II}
Show that any sequence of $n^3 + 1$ numbers contains either
\begin{itemize}
	\item a strictly-increasing subsequence of length $n + 1$.
	\item a strictly-decreasing subsequence of length $n + 1$, or
	\item $n + 1$ elements with the same value.
\end{itemize}
